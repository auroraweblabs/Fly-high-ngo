About us

Our vision

“No Old aged people are left behind without care. Our Foundation should take care the Old aged people whom does not have anyone to care for them”. Fostering a society wherein women are empowered enough to add value to their family and society; all children are empowered with education and enjoy their childhood rights, and creating sustainable and humane society wherein all living being have access to manage their livelihood.




Our Mission

Empowered women have a feminist ideology & wish to empower other deprived women .These women by associating with us whose mission is closely related to their ideology, will be able to realize their beliefs & this Will give them the higher level of satisfaction & accomplishment. Our primary mission is for earnings & independence of women. We provide progressive affirmative action programmes which helps many women in different sections to achieve social & financial status in india by providing entry into elite professions and the political arena we also pay more attention to the education & empowerment of underdeveloped.We provide platform,resources which include training programmes & also mentor services to promote their business. Furthermore There is a need for public support to enable wider diffusion of some of the key themes that are part of the feminist prospective ,namely, concern for equality and social justice this may increase the chances of success to those women who wish to become independent entrepruners.

Our Objective

“India becomes the best country in which to grow older”. Plan and implement or join hands with government, donors, non-government and other Volunteer bodies for implementing, development that fulfill our vision to educate , organize, strengthen the downtrodden communities with total capacities to manage their own development. We Believe in “Converts desires in to the reality… ” Fly high Foundation’s honest objective is to be there in all welfare and up-lift activities wherever & whenever society needs. Our foundation is to identify and fulfill the society’s basic needs.
